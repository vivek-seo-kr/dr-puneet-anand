About the Doctor

A Legacy of Excellence in Child Healthcare

Dr. Puneet Anand is a highly qualified Pediatrician and Neonatologist with extensive experience in managing complex pediatric cases and neonatal emergencies.

With a focus on evidence-based medicine and family-centered care, Dr. Anand has built a reputation for excellence in Delhi and the surrounding regions. He believes in building long-term relationships with families to ensure the best health outcomes for children.

  • Specialized training in Advanced Neonatal Life Support
  • Expertise in developmental and behavioral pediatrics
  • Committed to continuous medical education and research
